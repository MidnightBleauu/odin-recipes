Mango Mocktail

Declious Mango drink thats not a cocktail for the non-alocholic drinkers out there.

Mango Mocktail Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of fresh or frozen mango
  • 2 tablespoon of water
  • 1 lime or lemon
  • 6 mint leaves
  • optional extra lime or garnish

Steps

  1. Squeeze lime or lemon piece into a jar or pitcher using either a wooden spoon or muddler
  2. Clap mint together, add to the lime and give it a press with the muddler
  3. Add mango + water to a blender and blend to create mango puree
  4. Add puree to the lime and mint mixture, and stir.
  5. Fill glass with ice + sparkling water, and stir gently
  6. Garnish with mint and lime
  7. Enjoy !
