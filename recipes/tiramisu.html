100 Hours Tiramisu

Lady Finger Sponge Cake - Ingredients

  • 8 egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla
  • 8 Eggwhites
  • 1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1/3 + 1/2 cup of sugar, separated
  • 2 cups of flower
  • powdered sugar for dusting

Mascarpone Filling

  • 48 oz of heavy cream
  • 6 teaspoon lemon juice
  • zest of 1 lemon
  • 8 egg yolks
  • 1 cup + 1/4 or 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1.5 or 2 cups of heavy cream

Marsala / Caramel Sauce

  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/4 cup Marasala win (Brandy or Rum - 2.5 teaspoons
  • 4.5 teaspoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 teaspoon of sea salt or kosher salt
  • Note: If using marsala omit water, rum and brandy are stronger an less is needed

Baking Steps

    Homemade Mascarpone
  1. Pour heavy cream into a medium saucepan and bring to a simmer
  2. Once simmering, add lemon juice and whisk until the cream begins to thicken, roughly 13-15 minutes (don't let the cream get hotter than 190 fahrenheit while cooking).
  3. When thickened, turn off the heat and transfer the pan to an ice bath. Allow to cool for 10 minutes.
  4. Place a sieve lined with atleast 3-4 layers of cheesecloth in slightly large bowl, and transfer the cooled cream to the lined sieve. COver the cream and rest in the fridge for 24 hrs.
  5. After 24 hrs the cream will set. There will a little bit of liquid. Whats left in the cheese cloth will be

    6. Lady Finger Sponge Cake - Day One - Two
  6. For the lady finger sponge cake start with a hand mixer, beat the egg yolk, 1/3 cup sugar, and vanilla together until silky smooth
  7. In a separate clean bowl, and with beaters, mix the eggs whites and cream of tartar together and whip together. Gradually add 1/2 cup of sugar until it forms soft peaks.
  8. Combine whipped yolks and whites and beat until smooth. Gently fold using a spatula
  9. Spread batter evenly on an 18 x 13 baking sheet oiled and lined with parchment paper. Using a sieve dust powdered sugar. Immediately before baking on the bottom of the rack of an oven 375 fahrenheit for 12 - 15 minutes, until slightly golden.

    10. Marsala Carmel Sauce - Day Two
  10. Fit a pot with an inch of water and bring to a light simmer over medium heat
  11. With a heat-proof bowl, over the boiling water, add high quality egg yolks, sugar, and whisk constantly until warm.
  12. Use a hand mixer and beat on high until they are ligher in colour, and puffed up 2 times its original size.
  13. Whip the Mascarpone on medium until very smooth, add yolk mixture and gently fold. Ensure that there are no lumps.

    14. Bringing it all together
  14. Put the sponge cake in a 9"x13" baking dish in two layers.
  15. Combine cooled espresso, alcohol of your choice, sugar in a bowl and add about 1/4 of a cup to the bottom of the baking dish
  16. Add first cake layer, throughlly brush with coffee mixture. Drizzle with caramel sauce dust with cocoa powder, and grate a layer of choclate over the first layer.
  17. Add second layer to the baking dish, and drizzle the coffee mixture over cake with the cococa powder and grated chocolate.
  18. Repeat the layering process, and the steps above with coffee mixture, cocoa powder, and grated chocolate.
  19. Then it rest in the fridge for 24 hours.
  20. Enjoy your delcious tiramisu !
